About Safari Wheels

Safari Wheels is South Africa's premier car rental service, specializing in providing reliable vehicles for both tourists and locals. We take pride in our diverse fleet that caters to all your travel needs, from city exploration to wildlife safaris.

Founded in 2010, we've grown to offer service at all major airports and cities throughout South Africa. Our mission is to make your journey as smooth and enjoyable as possible.
